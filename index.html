Save Energy Consumption, Save Money with Smarthermo

Smarthermo, what is it?

Smarthermo is a device connected to an application that measures a room’s temperature on site. Thanks to the data storage and the heat map integrated into the application, it compares the given temperature to the city’s weather forecast and recommends a proper temperature to which you, as a user, can regulate your heating system in your house.

This application is implemented to you, dear citizens, in tracking your daily energy consumption behavior and to raise your awareness of the benefits of a sustainable lifestyle for the environment by suggesting optimal alternative solutions.

How to use it?

First, you have to take the Smarthermo device, turn it on and connect it to the dedicated application by Bluetooth. By clicking on each room's button, you will be able to see the energy consumption of the heater.

Then, you need to click on “Optimal Heater Temperature” and accept the app to have access to your location. The application will suggest the optimal heater temperature for that room, so you will know if you need to turn the heater down or not to save energy and money.

When clicking on "Insights" button, you will be provided with a better visualisation of rooms’ temperatures in your house thanks to the heat-map feature.

You will also be able to see how much energy you have saved and if that amount meets our target. Once there is an adequacy between those two, you will get a chance to have a 5€ voucher to spend at our partners' stores.

Our key features

Measuring

The device measures the room’s temperature on site and the application compares it to the city’s temperature

Estimating

The app can estimate the optimal temperature for each room in your house

Visualizing

It can also visualize temperatures of the heater in each room through a heatmap

Informing

The app provides personalized insights based on your history and updates on the latest developments in this field.
