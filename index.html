Save Energy Consumption, Save Money with Smarthermo

What is Smarthermo?

Smarthermo is an application that measures a room’s temperature on site. Smarthermo uses data storage and a heatmap which is integrated into the application. The heatmap compares the temperature of each room of your home to your city weather forecast thanks to geolocation. Thanks to your Smarthermo device, you are recommended the optimal temperature for which you can adjust your heater.

Smarthermo has partnered with Govee to bring you the ultimate thermo hygrometer that allows you to measure both air temperature and humidity. No more energy waste in your home!

Why should you use Smarthermo?

Smarthermo exists to ease people’s life by increasing their comfort during the cold months of winter. Our purpose is to help both the people and the environment by lowering CO2 emissions linked to excessive heating.

Who is Smarthermo for?

Everyone with a smartphone and a wifi connection! Smarthermo is made for anyone who wants to track their daily energy consumption habits. Whether it is to save the environment and raise your awareness about the benefits of a sustainable lifestyle or to save on your electricity bill, Smarthermo is the solution made for you!

How to use it?

If it is your first time using Smarthermo, you will need to design your very own home blueprint with heaters for Smarthermo to envision how your heating system works.

Once this is done and for all the times to come, you will do the following:

Our key features

Measuring

The device measures the room’s temperature on site and the application compares it to the city’s temperature

Estimating

The app can estimate the optimal temperature for each room in your house

Visualizing

It can also visualize temperatures of the heater in each room through a heatmap

Informing

The app provides personalized insights based on your history and updates on the latest developments in this field.
