What is Smarthermo?

Smarthermo is an application that measures a room’s temperature on site. It uses data storage and a heatmap which is integrated into the application. The heatmap compares the temperature of each room of your home to your city weather forecast thanks to geolocation. Now you can enjoy the optimal temperature for which you can adjust your heater!

Smarthermo has partnered with Govee to bring you the ultimate thermo hygrometer that allows you to measure both air temperature and humidity. No more energy waste in your home!

Why should you use Smarthermo?

Smarthermo exists to ease people’s life by increasing their comfort during the cold months of winter. Our purpose is to help both the people and the environment by lowering CO2 emissions linked to excessive heating.

Who is Smarthermo for?

Everyone with a smartphone and a wifi connection! Smarthermo is made for anyone who wants to track their daily energy consumption habits. Whether it is to save the environment and raise your awareness about the benefits of a sustainable lifestyle or to save on your electricity bill, Smarthermo is the solution made for you!

How to use it?

If it is your first time using Smarthermo, you will need to design your very own home blueprint with heaters for Smarthermo to envision how your heating system works. Once this is done and for all the times to come, let's follow these steps:

Step 1: Take the device, turn it on and connect it to the dedicated Smarthemo application through Bluetooth, allow the application to track your location to see the weather forecast in your position.

Step 2: Go to the room of your choice and click on the corresponding section that you would have determined in the blueprint at the beginning click on the “Measure Optimal Temperature” button to measure the temperature of the room.

Step 3: Et voilà! The screen displays your current room temperature and the “Optimal Heater Temperature” at which you should regulate your heater.

Our key features

Measuring & Estimating


The device measures the room’s temperature on site and the app estimates the optimal temperature for each room.

Visualizing


It can also visualize temperatures of all the heaters in your house through a heatmap.

Informing


The app provides personalized information based on your history and updates on the latest developments in this field.
