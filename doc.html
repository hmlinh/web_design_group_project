The United Nations Global Goals and choice of topic

In 2015, the United Nations General Assembly set up 17 interlinked global goals related to the environment’s preservation, entitled Global Goals, or the Sustainable Development Goals. They are intended to be achieved by 2030. In order for them to be achieved, all human beings around the world are asked to be aware of the environment’s protection, and how with the advent of renewable energies, factories, metamorphoses in terms of business and technical progress in general, growing economy all over the world is usually synonym of growing the impact on the environment. Climate change, growing extinction of fauna and flora, the appearance of pathogens and the multiplication of diseases are just the emerging part of the iceberg. Many activists have taken this matter into their own hands to defend the planet's interests, and as a part of this planet, it is our duty to have a positive impact on our environment too.

Directly related to Goal 8 of the Global Goals, our main topic is “Decoupling economic growth from environmental degradation”. The choice of this subject is based on the quick transformation business is going through all over the world and this includes the several implementations of new energies, overuse of natural resources and chemical reactions. This says, we are not against economic growth, however, we are looking for ways to boost the economy while preserving our environment.

Brainstorming & Research Question

In total adequacy with the Global Goals, and the Goal number 8 which hails our interest in a particular way, we have formulated together a research question which helped us brainstorm and find the perfect project idea: “How to raise citizens’ awareness about their daily consumption behaviour through the implementation of tools which lower their impact on the environment?”

Recently, having an eco-responsible profile became a lifestyle. Citizens who are adopting eco-friendly habits generally try to watch out for their actions when it comes to plastic waste, recycling products’ packaging and the list goes on. What we have noticed is that actions regarding the energies’ overuse are rare. Thus, we have decided to mainly tackle this point. From this brainstorming came the idea of reducing one’s energy consumption at home.

Birth of Smarthermo

As previously explained, the idea is to reduce our energy’s use at home. As we are in the core of winter, our heating at home never stops working. This reflection led us to the idea of reducing the energy used for heatings. In order to do so, we must not provide users with a temperature that keeps them freezing inside their houses, and at the same time, we shouldn’t over use our energy. This is why we decided to have a comparison between the temperature inside a room and the one outside, and to suggest the user the most optimal degree of temperature to regulate his heating

The name of the application “Smarthermo” came from the marriage of the digital transformation which demands using smart phones and devices, and the main actor in our idea which is the thermostat.

The main feature or activity of this project is to estimate the optimal temperature for each room in one’s house. As for the support activities, they are able to measure the room temperature on site, to visualize temperatures of the heater in each room through a heatmap, to provide the user with personalized insights based on his history and last, it offers useful tips and updates on latest developments in this field.

Application’s sketching

After finding the perfect idea for our project, imagining the process and giving it a name, it was time to come up with a 1st sketching. The latter involves the development of all screen images and pages possibly visited by the user in his phase of navigating on the application and checking all the features.

The 1st step was answering the main need which is estimating the optimal temperature in each room in the house. Then, the next features intervene too as completing activities and each one of them should be able to be presented in an individual page.
