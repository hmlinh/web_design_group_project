Let's discover our journey of creating Smarthermo!


The United Nations Global Goals and Choice of Topic

In 2015, the United Nations General Assembly set up 17 interlinked global goals related to the environment’s preservation, entitled Global Goals, or the Sustainable Development Goals. They are intended to be achieved by 2030.

In order for them to be achieved, all human beings around the world are asked to be aware of the environment’s protection through the advent of renewable energies, metamorphoses of businesses and overall progress.

Growing economy worldwide is usually synonymous with increasing the impact on the environment. Climate change, growing extinction of fauna and flora, the appearance of pathogens and the multiplication of diseases are just the emerging part of the iceberg. Many activists have taken this matter into their own hands to defend the planet's interests, and as a part of this planet, it is also our duty to have a positive impact on our environment.

Directly related to Goal 8 of the Global Goals, we focus on the following topic: “Decoupling economic growth from environmental degradation”. This subject is based on the quick transformation businesses are going through all over the world and this includes the several implementations of new energies, overuse of natural resources and chemical reactions. With this in mind, we are not against economic growth but we deeply think that economic development should not happen without environmental preservation.


Brainstorming & Research Question

In total adequacy with the Global Goals, and the Goal number 8 which hails our interest in a particular way, we have formulated together a research question which helped us brainstorm and find the perfect project idea: “How to raise citizens’ awareness about their daily consumption behaviour through the implementation of tools which lower their impact on the environment?”

Recently, having an eco-responsible profile became a lifestyle. Citizens who are adopting eco-friendly habits generally try to watch out for their actions when it comes to plastic waste, recycling products’ packaging and the list goes on. What we have noticed is that actions regarding the energies’ overuse are rare. Thus, we have decided to mainly tackle this point. From this brainstorming came the idea of reducing one’s energy consumption at home.

Design Thinking

As a team, we decided to apply the design thinking method to create our project. We followed the following steps:

  • Empathise: we looked at the current situation of a few big issues arising from businesses and their impact on society. We thought of food waste, climate change, wood industry, etc. We then thought of a big issue: heating, more precisely, heating in the European continent. We notice an important thing: most people spend a great deal of time adjusting their heater temperature due to temperature fluctuations.
  • Define: we started asking around ourselves who were the people facing this challenge but although it could be anyone, we decided to focus on a specific persona: young student females who are concerned about the environment.
  • Ideate: we decided to see what solutions could help them and we came up with a few ideas: a fitness app to track protein intake, a recycle code barre scanning app, and finally a thermo hygrometer to check the heating performances at home. Our targets being informed students, we wanted to create something that would help them track their energetic consumptions while also helping them with their finances.
  • Prototype: we created wireframes and then mockups to make our idea come true. We applied a few designs and collaborated on building it from scratch with the help of our research.
  • Test: we conducted a few user tests with profiles corresponding to our main target. We received feedback and adapted our prototype accordingly.

Birth of Smarthermo and Technology Behind It

As previously explained, the idea is to reduce our energy’s use at home. As we are in the cold wintertime, our heating at home never stops working. This reflection led us to the idea of reducing the energy used for heatings. In order to do so, we must not provide users with a temperature that keeps them freezing inside their houses, and at the same time, we shouldn’t overuse our energy. This is why we decided to have a comparison between the temperature inside a room and the one outside, and to suggest to the user the most optimal degree of temperature to regulate heating.

The name of the application “Smarthermo” came from the marriage of the digital transformation which demands using smartphones and devices, and the main actor in our idea which is the thermo hygrometer.

Having the knowledge to design the app, we decided to unite our forces with the company Govee which has now fusioned with Smarthermo. Govee used to only show air temperature and humidity. By associating with Smarthermo, Govee has won in technical know-how and extended its basic functionalities by implementing new features.

See how the previous model looked like:

The main feature of this project is to estimate the optimal temperature for each room in one’s house. Smarthermo includes an “insights" section and a heat map that you can personalize which will update itself automatically and let you see what your home looks like.

In the “extra” section, you can keep track of your progress by seeing how much energy you have saved. If the amount meets the target we have set, you will get a chance to have a 5 euros voucher to spend at our partners' stores. Our partners all are certified B corporations which respect the environment. If you wish, you may also use your voucher to make a donation to the Climate Justice Alliance (CJA*).

*CJA is a community of over 70 NGOs that focus on sustainability, development of underrepresented communities, race and ethnicity, economic development, poverty and alleviation, with the wider aim of addressing climate change.

Benchmark & Growth Opportunities

There are a few thermo hygrometers available on the market. They are mostly used to measure humidity and air temperature at a given time. However, none allows you to geolocate your environment to foresee what your heater temperature should be like based on your home blueprint.

Govee was one of those solutions but thanks to our partnership, we have been able to leverage their offer and to not have to produce devices which would have been an important cost for us.

Thanks to insights we have gained from the user tests, we will need to implement the following features after receiving fundraisings:

  • Make Smarthermo a solution adapted to both heater and air conditioner so seasonality does not impact our project too much.
  • Build smart heaters and smart plugs for people to buy a heater that can be modified remotely through the app and plugs that can adapt to any electric heater to establish a connection with other smart home devices compatible with Smarthermo app such as Alexa or Google Home.
